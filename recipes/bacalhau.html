Cooked Codfish with Potatoes

Description:

Codfish with potatoes is a portuguese dish, usually eaten on Christmas, but not exclusive.

Ingredients:

  • 4 pieces of cod
  • 1 Portuguese cabbage
  • 1kg potatoes
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 400ml olive oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Start by cutting the cabbage leaves in half and rinsing them in water.
  2. In a saucepan, bring the water, 2 bay leaves, cod, and cabbage to a boil. Season with salt.
  3. After boiling, let it cook for about 15 to 20 minutes, or until the cabbage is cooked and the cod is flaking.
  4. Wash the potatoes well and cook them in salted water for 30 minutes, or until cooked through. Remove from the heat and peel them. Set aside.
  5. In a frying pan, place the olive oil, the 2 bay leaves, and the peeled and crushed garlic cloves. Cook for 4 minutes. Discard the garlic and bay leaves.
  6. Place the cod fillets in a serving dish, arrange the cabbage around them, and finally add the boiled potatoes. Drizzle with the flavored olive oil and serve.
  7. Enjoy!
