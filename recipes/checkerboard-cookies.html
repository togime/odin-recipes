Checkerboard Cookies

Description:

Checkered cookies are decorative cookies, usually made around Christmas, combining a vanilla and chocolate flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 200g dairy butter
  • 250g wheat flour
  • 100g icing sugar
  • 2ts vanilla sugar
  • 2ts cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. Place room temperature butter, flour and icing sugar in a food processor and process the mixture until you get a nice, cohesive dough.
  2. Divide the dough in two. Knead one dough with vanilla sugar, and the other with cocoa powder.
  3. Divide the cocoa and vanilla dough into two pieces each. Roll out four long sausages.
  4. Place a vanilla and a cocoa sausage next to each other. Place the opposite on top of these to create a checkerboard pattern. Press the sausages together lightly and shape the sides straight by pressing each side lightly against the kitchen counter.
  5. Place plastic wrap around the dough and press the edges lightly to make a square. Place in the freezer for 15 minutes, or in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. This will make it easier to cut.
  6. Cut into slices about 1-2 cm thick. Bake the squares at 175 dgrees until they are golden and nicely browned, about 12 minutes. Let them cool.
  7. Enjoy!
