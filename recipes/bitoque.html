Bitoque

Description:

The bitoque is one of many well known portuguese dishes, composed of a simple steak, a side of fries and an egg on top.

Ingredients:

  • Beef steak
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • 2 or 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 or 2 bay leaves
  • Mustard
  • White wine
  • 1 Egg
  • Broth
  • Salt
  • Lemon

Steps:

  1. Start by pounding the steak with a meat mallet.
  2. Season with sliced garlic, salt, and bay leaves, then set aside.
  3. Meanwhile, cut the potatoes into very thin sticks.
  4. Wash the potatoes until the water runs clear.
  5. Drain the potatoes as best you can and fry them in hot oil until golden brown.
  6. Place the potatoes in absorbent paper, season with salt, and set aside.
  7. Meanwhile, heat a little olive oil in a frying pan.
  8. Add the butter and bay leaves and fry the steak on both sides over low heat.
  9. When the meat is almost cooked, add the mustard, beef stock, and white wine.
  10. Combine all ingredients and let the sauce reduce.
  11. Transfer the meat to a plate and pour the sauce over it.
  12. Fry an egg in a little olive oil and place it on top of the meat.
  13. Arrange the potatoes around the steak and add a slice of lemon.
  14. Enjoy!
